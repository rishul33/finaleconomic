SCAM Alert!
Please be watchful of rampant fraudulent scams using the name of the International Monetary Fund (IMF). The IMF serves its member countries with policy advice and capacity development services for their government entities and central banks. The Fund does not deal with personal businesses or with individuals. For more information, click here.

About OAP

As the IMF's window on the region, the Regional Office for Asia and the Pacific (OAP) contributes to economic surveillance and research, takes the lead in the IMF's on-the-ground involvement in regional cooperation, manages regional capacity building programs, and promotes the understanding and two-way dialogue of the IMF in Asia and the Pacific.

DIRECTOR BIO Chikahisa Sumi

IMF Capacity Development
in Asia and the Pacific

JAPAN-IMF Scholarship Program

Japan-IMF Scholarship Program for Asia (JISPA)

Scholarships for junior government officials from key economic agencies in Asia and the Pacific who wish to pursue graduate-level economics at Japanese universities.

Learn More


Japan-IMF Macroeconomic Seminar for Asia (JIMS)

The JIMS is an annual one-week seminar for senior Asian officials, which serves as a forum for current policy discussions.

Learn More


Japan-IMF Scholarship Program for Advanced Studies (JISP)

The Japan-IMF Scholarship Program for Advanced Studies (JISP) is a two-year program intended to help Japanese nationals obtaining Ph.D. degrees in macroeconomics and preparing them to work as economists at the International Monetary Fund (IMF).
