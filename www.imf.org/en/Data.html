Special Purpose Entities (SPEs)

The IMF released for the first time a new international database, which includes data on cross-border flows and positions of SPEs resident in 26 participating economies, based upon an international definition.

Climate Change Indicators Dashboard

The Dashboard is a single platform that brings together experimental climate change indicators and facilitates comparison across countries, developed in cooperation with other international organizations.

Financial Access Survey, 2021 Trends and Developments

Financial access remained strong in 2020 despite challenges posed by COVID-19, with an increased usage of digital financial services and traditional financial services remaining broadly stable.

Coordinated Direct Investment Survey

The IMF has released end-2020 results of the Coordinated Direct Investment Survey (CDIS). The CDIS is a global survey of cross-border direct investment holdings with immediate counterpart country information.
