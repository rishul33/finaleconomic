Papers and Notes

  • IMF Working Papers (WPs) are designed to make IMF staff research available to a wide audience. Nearly 300 working papers are released each year, covering a range of theoretical and analytical topics.

  • Departmental Papers focus on a specific economic topic, country or region.

  • Staff Discussion Notes (SDNs) showcase the latest policy related analysis and research by individual IMF staff and are published to encourage dialogue among a broad audience interested in economic policy issues.

  • The IMF Notes series offers practical advice and targeted analysis from IMF staff members to policymakers on important issues.
